Figma Project

Emergency Situaion Prototype App

Even though this project is only a prototype this is the possible solution to this problem: During emergencies, there is often a high demand for medical care, placing considerable pressure on the healthcare system. It is crucial to ensure access to medical equipment, personnel, and facilities, while also implementing precautionary measures to prevent the spread of illness.

Objectives in designing app

1. Alert and Notification System:
  • Implement a robust alert system that allows users to quickly send distress signals or emergency alerts to designated contacts or emergency services.
  • Consider incorporating various levels of alerts based on the urgency of the situation, enabling users to convey the severity of their emergency.
2. Emergency Contact and Communication:
  • Create a centralized database within the app where users can store emergency contacts, including family members, friends, and healthcare providers.
  • Enable direct communication channels (text, call, video) between users and emergency services or medical professionals for immediate assistance and guidance.
Location Services:
  • Utilize GPS technology to pinpoint the user's location accurately when an emergency is reported. This information can be crucial for emergency responders to reach the location swiftly.
  • Incorporate mapping features that guide emergency responders to the exact location of the user, especially in areas where addresses might not be easily identifiable.
4. User-Friendly Interface:
  • Simplify the user interface to ensure ease of use during high-stress situations. Use clear, intuitive design elements and minimal steps to request emergency assistance.
  • Implement visual cues or prompts to guide users through the app's functionalities, making it accessible even in stressful or panicked situations.
5. Precautionary Measures and Health Information:
  • Provide users with access to information on safety protocols and precautionary measures to take during emergencies (such as first aid tips or steps to prevent the spread of illness).
  • Integrate reliable health resources or guidelines from health organizations to educate users on best practices during emergencies.
6. Resource Allocation and Management:
  • Develop a system to monitor and manage the availability of emergency medical resources and personnel. This could involve real-time tracking of available ambulances, medical equipment, or healthcare professionals in the vicinity.
7. Data Security and Privacy:
  • Ensure robust security measures to protect user data and privacy, especially when dealing with sensitive medical information and emergency contacts.

Adruino Project

Automated Plant Watering System Using Microcontroller with SMS Notification

Despite the video being in Tagalog, here's an explanation in English: The Automated Plant Watering System operates by employing a microcontroller, soil moisture sensors, a water pump/valve, GSM module, and C++ programming language. This sophisticated system autonomously monitors soil moisture levels and administers water to plants when required. Its integrated capabilities include sending SMS notifications to users, providing real-time updates on the watering process. This functionality ensures optimal plant care and facilitates convenient remote monitoring, offering users peace of mind regarding their plants' well-being.
